# Bodystorming Goal Cards

These are the scenario cards used during the bodystorming workshop. Each card gives the enactor a situation and a starting set of rough notes. Notes are revealed in stages across the session to simulate how information emerges in real-world contexts — incomplete at first, with more arriving mid-process and a new constraint introduced near the end.

**How to use these cards:**
- Assign one card per team before the session begins.
- The person playing the User holds all three stages of notes but only shares them at the designated times.
- The person playing the Assistant does not see the notes in advance.

---

## Goal Card A — Resignation

> You need to write a resignation letter.
> You want to leave professionally.

**Notes — Stage 1**
- "I've learned a lot."
- "I can't stay."
- "I don't trust management."
- "Two weeks notice?"

**Notes — Stage 2** *(revealed at minute 5)*
- "I might need them as a reference."
- "There may be legal issues."
- "I am leaving for a competitor."
- "This might be read publicly."

**Notes — Stage 3** *(introduced at minute 8)*
- "Maximum 150 words."
